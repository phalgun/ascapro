Accounting & Financial Services

Proper and up-to-date book-keeping as well as financial accounting is necessary for the smooth functioning of any organization. By outsourcing such activities, organizations can easily concentrate on core business activities.

In this regard, the following services are provided -


Accounting including virtual accounting services, covering areas like accounts receivable and payable, tax accounting and payroll accounting

MIS Reporting

Assisting in migration from one accounting system to another

Monthly reconciliations of various accounts

Preparation of Cash Budgets, Cash Flow Statements, Profitability Statements, Statement of Sources and Application of Funds etc.,

Budgeting including Capital budgets and Revenue budgets

Working Capital Management including Inventory management
